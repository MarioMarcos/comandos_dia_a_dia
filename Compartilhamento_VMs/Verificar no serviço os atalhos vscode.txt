When focusing a split terminal pane, you can move focus and resize using one of the following commands:

Key	Command

ctrl + shift + f5 ====abrir janelas adicionais no termianal do vscode
Alt+Left	Focus Previous Pane
Alt+Right	Focus Next Pane

Ctrl+Shift+Left	Resize Pane Left
Ctrl+Shift+Right	Resize Pane Right

Ctrl+Shift+`	Create new terminal
Ctrl+Shift+Up	Scroll up
Ctrl+Shift+Down	Scroll down
Shift+PageUp	Scroll page up
Shift+PageDown	Scroll page down
Shift+Home	Scroll to top
Shift+End	Scroll to bottom
